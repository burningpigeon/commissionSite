GENERAL TERMS
  • My commissions can take anywhere to a couple days to 2-3 weeks. I'm a fulltime student with a part-time job so time can be hard to find.
  • Communcation is usually done through Discord, but I can also do email!
  • If you take a while responding to messages it may delay how long your commission takes.
  • Please provide a clear reference of your character. Include any additional details that are not shown on the reference!
  • You may ask for updates whenever, just as long as they are at least 48hrs apart
MY PROCESS
  • Usually we start off having a quick conversation about what you want your commission to look like, pose, expression etc.
  • I'll give you a WIP of the sketch, based on your feedback I can make edits to the sketch. Once approved I will move onto the lineart.
  • I will also send an image of the colours to verify that they are correct. Once approved, I'll finish up and we will be done!
PAYMENT
  • For commissions under 50$, payment can be made upfront or after the sketch
  • For commissions over 50$, payment can be made upfront or a 25% despoit with the rest after the sketch
  • I use PayPal invoices for all of my commissions, I can also take payment via Kofi
REFUNDS AND CANCELLATIONS
  • No refunds will be given after the work has been started. If only the sketch has been completed we might be able to negotiate a partial refund, however after that is on a case by case basis. After the sketch no refunds will be given.
  • If you make me feel uncomfortable or have creepy behavior, you will get one warning. If the behavior has not changed, you will be blacklisted and your order will be canceled
  • If you don’t reply to messages after 72 hours, you will get a warning message. If that message isn’t replied to within 48 hours, your order will be canceled. Please communcate if there is a reason you cannot reply for extended periods of time.
  • While this will not happen often, if I need to cancel the order due to personal issues you will get a FULL REFUND along with whatever work has been started.
ARTIST AND COMMISSIONER'S RIGHTS
  • If a sketch has been sent for approval, and the client refuses the sketch and requests an entirely new sketch, I have the right to edit the sketch and re-use the sketch.
  • I have a right to post any commissioned work on my social media, however you will still be credited as the owner of your character. If you don’t want your work posted please let me know in advance!
  • As the commissioner you have the right to post the commissioned piece wherever you’d like however please credit me when possible!
  • I have the right to refuse or deny any commission without explanation.​
